Sync your bank accounts to manage your finances effectively.

Added Accounts
    Sync Process
    1. Log in to your account.
    2. Select the accounts you want to sync from the dropdown.
    3. Click the "Add Account" button to add the selected account.
    4. Optionally, you can remove an added account by clicking the "Remove" button next to it.
    5. Once all desired accounts are added, click the "Sync Accounts" button to initiate the sync process.
    6. Wait for the process to complete.
    7. Your accounts are now synced and ready to use.
